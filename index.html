Even if you've never read this story or seen one of the countless adaptations, you likely know about the story of Alice and her adventures in Wonderland. The story of a girl following a rabbit down a rabbit-hole, she soon discovers a land where nothing quite makes sense. Here she encounters fascinating new characters, including a talking cat who can disappear and reappear.
